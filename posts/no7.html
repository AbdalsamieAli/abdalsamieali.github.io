  • Star-Delta Motor Startor


    2026-05-17

    I recently designed a Star-Delta Motor Starter using EPLAN Electric P8, focusing on developing a practical and standards-compliant motor starting solution.
    This project demonstrates how theoretical concepts in motor control can be translated into real-world industrial design. The system is structured to reduce inrush current during startup while ensuring safe and reliable operation.

    🔧 Key elements of the design include:

    • Complete power and control circuit development.
    • Interlocking logic for safe contactor operation.
    • Timer-based transition from star to delta configuration
    • Integration of thermal overload protection.
    • Clear and structured schematic documentation following IEC standards.

    ⚙️ Application:

    This type of starter is widely used in industrial environments for motors driving pumps, compressors, and conveyor systems where controlled startup is essential.
