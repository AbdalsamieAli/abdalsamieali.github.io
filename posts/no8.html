  • PER-UNIT SYSTEM


    2026-06-01

    The per-unit system expresses any quantity relative to a defined base value. Originally developed to simplify manual power system analysis, it's still widely used today — even in computer-based studies — to present results on a consistent, system-wide basis. Common applications include power flow, short circuit evaluation, and motor starting studies.
